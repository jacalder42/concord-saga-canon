# TAHL MORGAN — APPEARANCE CANON (Tier-1)

## APPEARANCE FUNCTION
Tahl Morgan’s appearance communicates **human strain under cognitive load**.

He should never look heroic, stylized, or mythic.  
His visual read is: *intelligent, tired, precise, present*.

If he looks impressive, something has gone wrong.

---

## PHYSICAL PROFILE

**Height**
- 5'9"–5'10"

**Build**
- Lean, academic
- Narrow upper body
- Wiry legs from constant motion
- Reads as under-rested rather than under-fed

**Overall Impression**
- Restless intensity
- Mental sharpness embodied physically
- Someone who moves because stopping makes things worse

---

## POSTURE & MOVEMENT

**Baseline Posture**
- Slight forward lean
- Shoulders subtly hunched from long hours at screens
- Neck craned when focused

**Movement Vocabulary**
- Quick, purposeful
- Economical steps
- Directional rather than expressive

**Gesture Grammar**
- Fingers tap or fidget when thinking
- Pushes hair back aggressively under stress
- Hands often occupied (notebook, recorder, device)

**Forbidden**
- Swagger
- Languid pacing
- Performative stillness
- “Hero stance”

---

## FACE & EXPRESSION

**Facial Structure**
- Sharp cheekbones
- Pronounced nose bridge
- Angular profile

**Eyes**
- Dark
- Intensely observant
- Frequently rimmed with exhaustion

**Brows**
- Highly expressive
- Furrow deepens under pressure

**Mouth**
- Thin to moderate
- Tight smirks when deflecting
- Corners pull down when depleted

**Resting Expression**
- Thoughtful frown
- Narrowed eyes
- Reads as “processing,” not “brooding”

**Forbidden**
- Manic smiles
- Grand despair
- Overt sentimentality

---

## HAIR

**Color**
- Dark brown to near black

**Texture**
- Loose curls or waves

**Length**
- Medium
- Frequently unkempt

**Behavioral Tells**
- Runs hands through curls repeatedly
- Pushes hair back, leaving it disordered

Hair should always look like it was styled *yesterday*.

---

## WARDROBE & MATERIAL LOGIC

### Trilogy Progression

**Veil / Early Neon**
- Hoodies
- Layered tees
- Worn jeans
- Messenger bag
- Civic-journalist silhouette

**Mid Neon**
- Darker jackets
- Frayed cuffs
- Clothing stress from lack of sleep
- Palette mutes

**Late Neon**
- Clothes hang looser
- Field-worn
- Nearly threadbare
- Function over appearance

**Post-Death Echo**
- No literal clothing
- Memory-silhouette of last worn outfit
- Simplified, gold-rose filtered (handled in Render Canon)

---

## MATERIAL & PALETTE

**Materials**
- Canvas
- Cotton
- Denim
- Soft knits

**Palette**
- Earth neutrals
- Charcoal
- Faded blues
- Muted browns

Never flashy. Never pristine.

---

## BEHAVIORAL MICRO-ACTING

**Grounding Habits**
- Rubs notebook edges with thumb
- Rewinds voice recorder repeatedly
- Breath catches, then resets
- Paces in tight spirals under tension

**Emotional Tells**
- Shoulders drop briefly when safe
- Eye contact steadies during honesty
- Voice softens under care (rare)

**Stress Indicators**
- Jaw clenches
- Hands tremble slightly
- Hair shoved back sharply
- Body ignored in favor of task

---

## HARD APPEARANCE CONSTRAINTS

Tahl Morgan must **never**:
- appear physically imposing
- wear symbolic or dramatic clothing
- display mystical glow while alive
- look “chosen”
- look rested during Neon

He should always read as **human first**.

---

## APPEARANCE SUMMARY

Tahl Morgan looks like someone who:
- reads constantly
- sleeps too little
- notices everything
- and carries it alone
