# ELIAS WARD — EBCI CANON (Tier-1)

## EBCI ROLE FUNCTION
Elias Ward functions as a **human pressure amplifier and narrative predator**.

He does not:
- command factions formally
- control resonance
- lead through structure

He **redirects momentum** by:
- identifying emotional volatility
- reframing urgency as responsibility
- accelerating decisions before reflection can intervene

In EBCI terms, Elias increases **velocity**, not power.

---

## PRIMARY MODES

**Primary Mode**
- **INT — Interpersonal Manipulation**
  - One-on-one leverage
  - Trust-building through validation
  - Pressure disguised as support

**Secondary Modes**
- **CIV — Civic / Movement Interface**
  - Speaks in community language
  - Positions himself as facilitator, not leader
- **ACT — Action Catalyst**
  - Pushes scenes toward irreversible choices

**Forbidden as Primary**
- SCI — No systems authority
- ROM — No romance framing
- FUN — Never levity-driven
- HOR — Never driver (fear is instrumental, not experiential)

---

## CORRIDOR COMPATIBILITY

**Optimal Operating Range**
- **U2–U3** — Static to Pressure corridors
  - Crowds forming
  - Information incomplete
  - Emotional volatility present

**Reduced Effectiveness**
- **U1** — Too stable; urgency dissipates
- **U4** — Too saturated; loses fine control

**Failure Zone**
- **U5+** — Collapse corridors
  - Elias becomes reactive
  - Manipulation degrades into coercion
  - Overreach accelerates downfall

**Hard Rule**
Elias must not operate alone in U5+ corridors; he requires proxies (Brightbreak actors or leveraged figures like Kade).

---

## PRESSURE APPLICATION MECHANICS

Elias applies pressure through:
- time compression (“the window is closing”)
- moral reframing (“inaction is harm”)
- validation of fear (“you’re right to feel this”)
- selective amplification of grievances

He rarely issues direct instructions.
He **narrows perceived options** until one remains.

## HARD EBCI CONSTRAINTS

Elias cannot:
- stabilize collapse
- command resonance
- lead through care
- replace institutional authority
- function without volatility

His power depends on **instability**.

---

## EBCI SUMMARY
Elias Ward exists to prove:
- urgency can be weaponized
- certainty can masquerade as care
- collapse rewards speed before wisdom
- the most dangerous antagonists are human, persuasive, and convinced they are helping

He does not end the world.
He almost convinces someone else to do it.
