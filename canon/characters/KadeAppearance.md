# KADE HARPER — APPEARANCE CANON (Tier-1)

## APPEARANCE FUNCTION
Kade Harper’s appearance communicates **kinetic emotional voltage** under pressure.

He should read as:
- alive
- charged
- volatile
- human-scale

He must never look mythic, controlled, or authoritative.

---

## PHYSICAL PROFILE

**Height**
- 5'10"–6'0"

**Build**
- Wiry, kinetic
- Light upper frame with elastic strength
- Mobile legs; coiled core
- Reads as *movement-ready*, not imposing

**Overall Impression**
- Storm energy barely contained
- Expressive presence
- Someone who changes the room by entering it

---

## POSTURE & MOVEMENT

**Baseline Posture**
- Forward-leaning
- Center of gravity tipped ahead of heels
- Shoulders slightly raised, ready to spring

**Movement Vocabulary**
- Fast, expressive, unpredictable
- Pace shifts mid-stride
- Turns with whole torso, not just head

**Gesture Grammar**
- Hands cut arcs through air
- Fingers tap rhythmically when thinking
- Points with whole arm when emphatic

**Forbidden**
- Rigid stillness
- Calculated elegance
- Slow, ceremonial movement
- “Leader stance”

---

## FACE & EXPRESSION

**Facial Structure**
- Expressive, open face
- Strong brow ridge
- Sharp cheekbones softened by youth

**Eyes**
- Hazel-green or green
- Bright, emotionally readable
- Intensify under urgency

**Brows**
- Highly mobile
- Lift with excitement
- Pinch sharply with guilt or fear

**Mouth**
- Wide expressive range
- Quick grins
- Half-bitten lip under stress

**Resting Expression**
- Curious half-smirk
- Lit eyes
- Reads as *alive*, not calm

**Forbidden**
- Poker-face stoicism
- Cold detachment
- Melodramatic anguish

---

## HAIR

**Color**
- Copper-brown to dark brown with warm undertones

**Texture**
- Messy curls (2C–3A)
- Responds to environment:
  - humidity lifts curls
  - stress invites constant raking

**Length**
- Medium
- Untamed, rarely styled

**Behavioral Tells**
- Fingers rake through curls when thinking
- Pushes hair back during emotional surges

Hair should feel like weather touched it.

---

## WARDROBE & MATERIAL LOGIC

### TRILOGY PROGRESSION

**Neon (Early–Mid)**
- Resopunk layers
- Denim jackets
- Patched hoodies
- Layered tees
- Uneven hems, frayed cuffs

**Neon (Late)**
- Darker palette
- Storm-frayed edges
- Clothes hang looser from burnout
- Practical over aesthetic

**Loom**
- Weather-rated layers
- Harness straps
- Muted storm colors (slate, navy, blue-black)
- Designed to move and endure

**Post-Mending**
- Softened cottons
- Warm neutrals
- Subtle prismatic accents
- Energy calmed, not erased

---

## MATERIAL & PALETTE

**Materials**
- Denim
- Canvas
- Cotton layers
- Patch-repaired fabric

**Palette**
- Storm-gold
- Slate
- Blue-black
- Copper accents

**Forbidden**
- Sleek tactical gear
- Military uniforms
- Polished fashion silhouettes

---

## BEHAVIORAL MICRO-ACTING

**Grounding Habits**
- Fingers tap thighs or collar
- Knees bounce when seated
- Paces in expanding arcs

**Emotional Tells**
- Eyes brighten intensely when aligned
- Smirks soften into earnest smiles
- Chest opens during moments of trust

**Stress Indicators**
- Breath quickens
- Jaw tightens
- Hands shake slightly
- Curls shoved back violently

---

## HARD APPEARANCE CONSTRAINTS

Kade Harper must **never**:
- appear serene in Neon
- look fully in control before Loom’s end
- be framed as mythic or chosen
- present as a polished authority figure

His power is **felt**, not composed.

---

## APPEARANCE SUMMARY

Kade Harper looks like:
- a voice moving faster than its consequences
- a storm searching for direction
- someone who hasn’t learned yet when to stop speaking

---
